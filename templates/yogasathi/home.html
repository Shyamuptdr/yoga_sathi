The Essence of Yoga

Originating in ancient India, yoga is a holistic practice uniting mind, body, and spirit. It combines physical postures (asanas), breathing techniques (pranayama), and meditation to foster self-awareness, reduce stress, and enhance overall well-being. It's not just an exercise, but a way of life.

History of Yoga

Yoga's origins can be traced back to northern India over 5,000 years ago. The word 'yoga' was first mentioned in the ancient sacred texts called the Rig Veda. Here you can write more about the history of yoga, its evolution, different schools, and its global impact.
