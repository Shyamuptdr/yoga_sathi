About Yoga Sathi

Yoga Sathi is a web-based platform that uses your webcam and computer vision to provide real-time feedback on your yoga posture, helping you practice safely and effectively.

Our mission is to make yoga accessible to everyone, everywhere, with the help of modern technology. We believe in the power of yoga to improve physical and mental well-being.
